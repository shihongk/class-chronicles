# S3: Quiet Class — Lower Sec Math

## Scenario Profile
**Level**: Lower Sec  
**Class Profile**: Quiet Class  
**Auto-derived**: Mathematics / Linear equations  

**Core tension**: High compliance/low participation; surface answers, no reasoning/debate. Surface understanding vs silence? [file:1][file:4][file:5]

## Episode 1 — The moment you see the gap

**Setup**:  
> 22 mins into Sec 2 Normal Math on linear equations. Modelled "Twice Ali's age + 4 = Sam's age; Sam = 20. Find Ali." → Ali=8. Set 3 problems. Circulated.

**Focal student reactions**:  
- Rajan: correct fast, silent.  
- Nur: correct, stares ahead.  
- Wei Jie: correct slow, head down.  
- Siti: blank page, looks around.

**Question**: What first? 18 mins left. [file:3][file:6]

### Choice A: Thumbometer + Two-Step¹²
**Description**: "Thumbs: confident? Now Q1: factors? Why setup equation?"  
**Trade-off**: Quick silence gauge + reasoning check, minimal disruption.¹ [file:6]

### Choice B: Paired think-aloud (ZPD prompts)³⁴
**Description**: Shoulder pairs: "Explain equation to partner silently first." Prompts for stalled.  
**Trade-off**: Breaks silence safely (respectful), pairs uneven.³ [file:4]

### Choice C: Accountable Talk sentence stems⁵⁶
**Description**: "I think equation because... Partner: I agree/disagree because..." Circulate IRF.  
**Trade-off**: Forces justification, overwhelms quiet.⁵ [file:5][file:6]

## Episode 2A (After Thumbometer MCQ)

**Consequence**:  
> 6 up (confident), 14 side (unsure), 4 down. MCQ: 16 correct setup, 8 justify. Nur correct/no why. Rajan up/both. Siti down/blank. Silence pattern clear. 16 mins left. [file:3][file:6]

### A1: AQS on unsure thumbs⁸¹
**Description**: Unsure thumbs AQS partner's MCQ ("Affirm setup, Question why, Suggest check").  
**Trade-off**: Feedback practises talk, confident disengage.⁸ Serves: unsure. Underserves: confident. [file:6]

### A2: IRF chains on justifications⁶⁹
**Description**: "Why that setup? What if +2?" Probe 3-4 volunteers.  
**Trade-off**: Deepens select voices, others passive.⁶ Serves: volunteers. Underserves: silent majority. [file:5]

## Episode 2C (After Accountable Talk Stems)

**Consequence**:  
> 8 pairs "agree because works", 6 silence, 4 probe ("disagree, constants wrong"). Rajan leads. Siti mumbles. Shallow claims surface. 16 mins left. [file:3][file:5]

### C1: Gallery critique AQS⁵⁸
**Description**: Post stems anonymously; AQS weakest ("Affirm claim, Question because, Suggest alt").  
**Trade-off**: Structured peer talk on silence output, voting biases.⁵ Serves: emerging talkers. Underserves: non-writers. [file:6]

### C2: Hattie task feedback reteach⁷¹²
**Description**: Board common shallow claim; task-level reteach ("Your setup correct because balances").  
**Trade-off**: Directs silence to depth, less student voice.⁷ Serves: shallow majority. Underserves: confident. [file:5]
